About Us

NACABS Polytechnic, Kabayi Study Center is a government-accredited institution committed to delivering high-quality technical and vocational education. Since our inception, we have produced competent graduates ready to take on leadership roles in industry, entrepreneurship, and research. Our philosophy is rooted in innovation, discipline, and academic excellence.

Our Mission

To provide world-class education and hands-on training that equip students with practical skills, ethical values, and problem-solving capabilities to contribute meaningfully to national development.

Our Vision

To be a leading polytechnic recognized globally for producing innovative, employable, and entrepreneurial graduates.

Our Core Values

  • Academic Excellence
  • Integrity and Accountability
  • Innovation and Creativity
  • Student-Centered Learning
  • Community Engagement

Accreditation

NACABS Polytechnic, Kabayi Study Center is accredited by the National Board for Technical Education (NBTE), with full recognition by the Federal Ministry of Education. Our programs meet industry and academic standards.

Leadership

Our institution is governed by a team of experienced educators and administrators who are passionate about student success. The Rector, alongside dedicated Deans and Department Heads, ensures we uphold our academic and moral standards.
