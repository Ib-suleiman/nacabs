Our Academic Structure

NACABS Polytechnic, Kabayi Study Center offers a wide range of National Diploma (ND) and Higher National Diploma (HND) programs designed to equip students with technical knowledge, practical skills, and industry readiness. Our curriculum is developed in alignment with NBTE standards and regularly updated to meet market demands.

  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Computer Engineering

School of Environmental Studies

  • Building Technology

Academic Calendar

Our academic session is divided into two semesters: First Semester (October–February) and Second Semester (March–July), with mid-semester and end-of-semester examinations. Continuous assessment is integrated to ensure consistent academic monitoring.

Examination & Grading System

Students are evaluated through tests, assignments, and final exams. The grading system follows the standard polytechnic grading scale approved by NBTE, ensuring transparency and merit-based progression.
