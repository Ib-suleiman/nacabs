Admission Overview

NACABS Polytechnic welcomes applications from qualified candidates seeking National Diploma (ND) and Higher National Diploma (HND) programs. We are committed to providing a transparent and merit-based admission process.

Entry Requirements

  • Minimum of 5 O'Level credits in relevant subjects including English and Mathematics (for ND programs).
  • OND/ND certificate from a recognized institution with at least Lower Credit (for HND programs).
  • JAMB UTME result with the Polytechnic selected as first or second choice (for ND applicants).

Application Process

  1. Visit our official admissions portal.
  2. Fill out the online application form.
  3. Pay the non-refundable application fee.
  4. Upload necessary documents (e.g. O'Level results, birth certificate).
  5. Submit and await confirmation via email/SMS.
